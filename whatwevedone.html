What We've Done

October 28, 2017

Eunoia's first ever benefit concert. What a successful and joyful night it was. With the help of talented teen musicians as well as generous supporters we were able to fundraise over $2000! Thank you to all who helped out!

October 21, 2016

Donated school supplies to Luther Burbank High School. It was Eunoia's first official community outreach event, and it was a success.
