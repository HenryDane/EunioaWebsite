How You Can Help

Currently, as we are in the process of growing a small, local non-profit, all financial donations to help us grow and reach out to more teens in the community are so greatly appreciated. All donations are so greatly appreciated, and our promise is that every single dollar will be going towards teens.

The big difference that we make, couldn't happen without you. Please consider make a donation that would benefit not just us in helping to make a difference but also teens who are in need in the Sacramento community.

Volunteers are how organizations ours flourish and do well. We honestly couldn't do it without you. Volunteers are a real gift and we genuinely appreciate all they do.

Volunteering is half of what this organization is about. And if you're a teen that would like to give back to your community, you've come to just the right place.

Your generosity means so much and it makes that much of a positive difference. Thank you.

Another way you can help is by volunteering! We love love love our volunteers as they are what makes the world go 'round. If you're interested in volunteering, please do fill out the contact box below and we will get back to you as soon as we can. You're amazing! :)
